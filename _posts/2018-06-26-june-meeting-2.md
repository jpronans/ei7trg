The next meeting of the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group will take place in The [Clonmel Park Hotel](http://www.clonmelparkhotel.com/), Clonmel on Thursday 28th June at 8.30pm. Preparation will be made for participation in events taking place over the summer months. New members or anyone interested in Amateur Radio are very welcome to attend. For anyone who wishes to find out more about the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group and its activities you can come along to any of their meetings or contact club PRO Ronan EI4KN at QTHR for further information.
