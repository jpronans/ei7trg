The Annual General Meeting of the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group will be held at [The Clonmel Park Hotel](https://www.clonmelparkhotel.com/), Clonmel on Tuesday 15th January at 8pm.

  
This is the most important meeting of the calendar year and all group members are requested to attend. Short Wave Listeners are in particular very welcome. A number of topics are up for discussion as well as making plans for 2019.
