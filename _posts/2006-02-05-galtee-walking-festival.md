Tipperary Amateur Radio Group will be participating in the Glen of Aherlow Walking festival on Saturday and Sunday the first and second of April 2006. It is hoped that both APRS and Voice will be used over the weekend to monitor the progress of the walks. If anyone is interested in helping out over the weekend, please contact John, EI7IG at the following co-ordinates. ei7ig at ei3dib.#23.irl.ie, ei7ig at ei7trg dot org or at 086 8167310.
