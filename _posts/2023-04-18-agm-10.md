he Annual General Meeting of the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group will be held at [The Park Hotel](https://www.clonmelparkhotel.com/), Clonmel on Wednesday 19th April at 8pm.

This is the most important meeting of the calendar year and all members are requested to attend. Short Wave Listeners and new members are in particular very welcome. The election of club officers will take place as well as making plans for the coming year.

For more information about Tipperary Amateur Radio Group you can contact the club chairman Andy EI5JF at QTHR.
