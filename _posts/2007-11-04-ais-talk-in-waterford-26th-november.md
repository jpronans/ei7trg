<span class="postbody">Cormac EI4HQ will give a talk to [SEARG](http://www.searg.com) about AIS (Automatic Identification System), a recently introduced radio based ship transponder technology. AIS is quickly becoming an indispensable information &amp; safety tool at sea. Cormac will describe why AIS is interesting from a radio point of view, and he will suggest a few ways it can be of use in amateur radio applications.</span>AIS: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Automatic\_Identification\_System](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Automatic_Identification_System)South Eastern Amateur Radio Group meet at the Roanmore Social and Sports Centre, Cleaboy Road, Waterford.
