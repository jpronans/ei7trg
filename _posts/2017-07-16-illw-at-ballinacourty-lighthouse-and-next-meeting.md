Tipperary Amateur Radio Group will activate [Ballinacourty Lighthouse](http://www.irishlights.ie/tourism/our-lighthouses/ballinacourty-point.aspx) (ILLW Reference: IE0018) on Saturday and Sunday 19th &amp; 20th August 2017 as part of the 20th International Lighthouse &amp; Lightship weekend. Ballinacourty Lighthouse is located on the South coast of Ireland in County Waterford. The light at Ballinacourty was first established on 1st July 1858. The club will use the callsign EI7T for the activation. The July meeting of the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group will take place in The Park Hotel, Clonmel on Thursday 27th July at 8.30pm. New members or anyone interested in amateur radio are very welcome to attend.
