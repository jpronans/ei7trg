The Annual General Meeting of the Tipperary Amatuer Radio Group will be held at The[ Park Hotel](http://www.clonmelparkhotel.ie), Clonmel on Tuesday the 6th of December 2016 at 8pm. This is the most important meeting of the calender year and all group members are asked to attend. Short Wave Listeners are in particular very welcome. A number of radio related topics are up for discussion as well as plans for participation in up coming events for 2017.
