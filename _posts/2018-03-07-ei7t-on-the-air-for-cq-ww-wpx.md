Olivier ON4EI, is back again in Ireland from 18th to 28th March 2018 to operate during the CQ WPX SSB contest. He will be using [EI7T](https://www.qrz.com/db/EI7T) for the duration of the contest. Olivier is running contests in Low Power/Single Op SSB category using mainly green energy with his temporary 'Field Day like' antenna park setup.

- Inverted L 16m V/24m H monopole for 160 &amp; 80m + 32 radials.
- Inverted V dipole for 160-80m-40m bands.
- 80m long beverage RX antenna East-West directional.
- Spiderbeam &amp; Mosley TA33Jr antennas for 20/15/10m bands.
- Transceivers: 2 x Elecraft K3 + P3 SO2R 100W.
- 500Ah battery bank + 180W solar panel and 400W wind generator.

You can follow this one-man expedition on [qrz.com](https://www.qrz.com/db/EI8GQB) or meet him on the air.
