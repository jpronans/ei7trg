The clubs first meeting of the year will take place Thursday next, February 22nd at The [Clonmel Park Hotel](https://www.clonmelparkhotel.com/). New members or anyone interested in amateur radio are very welcome to attend. For anyone who wishes to find out more about the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group and its activities you can come along to any of their meetings, or contact club PRO Ronan, EI4KN at QTHR for further information.
