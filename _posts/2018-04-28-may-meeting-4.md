The next meeting of the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group will take place in Thursday next, 3rd May at 20:30 in the [Clonmel Park Hotel](https://www.clonmelparkhotel.com/). Plans will be finalised for events taking place over the summer months. New members or anyone interested in amateur radio are very welcome to attend. For anyone who wishes to find out more about the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group and its activities you can come along to any of their meetings, or contact club PRO Ronan, EI4KN at QTHR for further information.
