The next meeting of the Tipperary Amateur Radio Group will be held at The [Talbot Hotel](https://www.talbothotelclonmel.ie/), Clonmel on Wednesday the 14th of June 2023 at 8pm. This Hotel was formerly known as the Clonmel Park Hotel. The meeting is open to all and Short Wave Listeners are in particular very welcome to attend.
